This app is used to cut a board as would do a Chop Saw or a Miter Saw. If an input is not a number, it will be converted as zero or the most close number.

Settings

  • Unit: Chose the unit used.
  • Pixels for an inch: Chose how much pixel represents an inch on the screen.
  • Bit Diameter: The diameter of the bit of the machine.
  • Safe Z: the height between the board support and the lower bit position.

Miter

  • Miter angle: the angle of the miter.
  • Tilt: the direction of the tilt.

Board

  • Board length: the board length.
  • Cut length (back edge): the length of the back edge after the cut (represented on the top in blue).
  • Cut length (front edge): the length of the front edge after the cut (represented on the bottom in green).
  • Reverse the cut direction: chose if this is the front edge or the back edge which is modified.
  • Board thickness: the board thickness.
  • Cut height (from tabletop): the cut height.
  • Number of Cutting Passes: the number of cutting passes the machine will do.

Cut

Chose if the miter passes on the center of the cut or on its right.
